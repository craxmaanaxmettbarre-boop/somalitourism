Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Somalia?

Travel requirements vary by nationality and current regulations. Please check official guidance before booking.

What is the best time to travel?

Dry seasons are often preferred for coastal and outdoor travel, while local conditions may vary.

Can I book a custom tour?

Yes. Our team can help organize tailored package options for families, couples, and small groups.

Travel Tips

  • Carry valid identification and emergency contacts.
  • Pack light layers and sun protection.
  • Respect local customs and community guidance.
